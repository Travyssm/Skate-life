Come and skate with us!

A community led skate group



SkateLife is a group for skaters that currently holds meetings based in West Bromwich and Birmingham. Created so that skaters of all skill levels can gather in order to skate park trails and attend roller discos together.

Skating is a great way to improve aerobic fitness and cardiovascular health, while also aiding in lower body strength and endurance. Rolling with us is a great way to meet new people and potential life long friends.
